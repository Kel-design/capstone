Our Story

We hope you find joy in our candles during life's happiest and saddest times!

How We Began

During the great freeze of February 2020 our Austin home was without power for 8 straight days! While passing the cold and dark days our family vowed to learn to make candles to never run out of light again. The project became a passion, we now love sharing our wonderful candles with the world!

Meet the Team
